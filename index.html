Murakami Haruki (村上春樹)

Novels

Hear the Wind Sing

1979 | 🐱 7.0/10

Synopsis: A college student on summer break returns to his hometown and reconnects with his aloof friend at the local bar.

Thoughts: This novel doesn't seem to get a lot of love, even from Murakami fanatics, but I'm actually quite fond of it. The atmosphere is exquisite, and although Murakami himself may think differently, I don't see much of a gap between this work and his later works.
